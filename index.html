About

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring Remastered

The Lord of the Rings is a film series consisting of three high fantasy adventure films directed by Peter Jackson. They are based on the novel The Lord of the Rings by J. R. R. Tolkien.

15 years ago The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring was released. For this anniversary, we are releasing the movie remastered that features more than 2 hours of extended scenes that were carefully selected under the supervision of director Peter Jackson. Also, Exclusive 2017 interviews with the cast featuring Elijah Wood, Viggo Mortensen, Orlando Bloom, Sean Astin and many more. Fans of Middle-earth will have the opportunity to re-live the epic journey with Frodo and Sam to Mordor.
